Hamburgers

A delicious hamburger made with love and TLC

Hamburgers are one of most popular dishes in the entire world and it's so easy to make! All you need is two buns, beef patty, lettuce, cheese, onions, and tomatoes.

It's a simple go-to meal and is a timeless classic that is always loved by everone.

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Beef patty
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • onions
  • pickles

Steps

  1. First, cook the beef patty on a stove to your preferred temperature
  2. Toast the buns and add mayonaise on one side
  3. Place the finished patty in between the buns and add the rest of the ingredients to stack on it
  4. Let it rest and enjoy!
